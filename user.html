Help & Support
How do I upload a prescription?

Click the "Upload Rx" button on the home page, drag/drop or choose a clear image of your doctor's prescription. Our HIPAA pharmacists will verify it in minutes.

What are the payment options?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD) as well as secure online payments via Razorpay / UPI.

How long does verification take?

Prescription verifications usually conclude in 5-15 minutes. You'll get real-time push alerts as soon as it's completed.
